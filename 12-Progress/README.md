# 📈 Progress Tracker

Consistency is the key to mastering programming and succeeding in technical interviews. This section helps you track your learning journey, monitor progress, identify weak areas, and stay accountable throughout your preparation.

Whether you're preparing for placements, coding interviews, or competitive programming, maintaining a structured record of your progress helps build discipline and measure improvement over time.

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

By using this section, you will be able to:

- Track your daily and weekly learning progress.
- Monitor solved problems and contest performance.
- Plan revisions effectively.
- Stay organized throughout interview preparation.
- Measure consistency and long-term improvement.

# 🚀 Why Track Your Progress?

A structured progress tracker helps you:

- Build consistent study habits.
- Stay motivated throughout your preparation.
- Identify weak areas early.
- Reinforce previously learned concepts through revision.
- Prepare systematically for interviews.
- Measure improvement over time.

---

# 💡 Best Practices

- Update your logs every day.
- Review your progress at the end of each week.
- Revise difficult topics regularly.
- Analyze every contest-not just your rating.
- Revisit unsolved and challenging problems.
- Celebrate milestones to maintain motivation.

---

# ⭐ End Goal

By the end of your preparation journey, you should be able to:

- Maintain a consistent learning routine.
- Track your strengths and identify weak areas.
- Measure progress using meaningful metrics.
- Stay organized throughout interview preparation.

A well-maintained progress tracker transforms preparation into a structured, measurable journey and helps you stay focused until you achieve your placement or career goals.
